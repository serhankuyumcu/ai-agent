With AI, you can implement customer service (AI chatbot), content creation, data analysis, pricing optimization, and process automation in your business. For businesses in tourism, AI-powered reservation systems, multi-language support, and personalized marketing provide great advantages. As AI-Agent, we develop custom AI solutions for your business.

Our AI trainings range from 1-day workshops to 3-month comprehensive programs. Web design prices vary depending on the scope of the project. A basic corporate site, e-commerce site, or custom software project will have different costs. As AI-Agent, we offer free price quotes. A simple corporate website takes 2-3 weeks, a mid-sized e-commerce site 4-6 weeks, and complex custom software projects can take 2-3 months. The duration depends on content preparation, design revisions, and special functions. As AI-Agent, we guarantee fast delivery and allow you to track the project process with weekly reports.
9. Why is a mobile-responsive website important? +
About 80% of internet users access via mobile devices. Especially in the tourism sector, tourists search from mobile devices. A non-mobile-compatible site both worsens user experience and performs poorly in Google rankings. As AI-Agent, we design all our websites with a mobile-first approach.
10. What should be considered in hotel website design? +
For hotel websites, online booking systems, room galleries, price calendars, multi-language support (especially English, German, Russian), virtual tours, customer reviews, and mobile optimization are critical. Integration with booking platforms and fast loading times are also important. As AI-Agent, we offer web design solutions specific to the tourism sector.
11. How long does it take for SEO to show results? +
SEO work generally starts to show tangible results within 3-6 months. Technical improvements and content optimization are done in the first 1-2 months, rankings start to rise in the 3rd-4th month, and stable traffic increase is seen after the 6th month. Being patient is important. As AI-Agent, you can track your progress with monthly reports.
12. What is Local SEO and why is it important? +
Local SEO ensures your business appears at the top of search results in a specific geographical area. For searches like "plumber near me" or "restaurant in Antalya", Google My Business optimization, registration in local directories, and region-specific content creation are critical. Local SEO is essential to reach local customers.
13. How is AI SEO done? +
AI-powered SEO uses AI tools for content optimization, keyword research, competitor analysis, and user behavior prediction. As AI-Agent, we determine the most effective keywords with machine learning, automate content suggestions, and adapt quickly to Google algorithm changes. This way, you can get ahead of your competitors.
14. Why is Google My Business important? +
Google My Business (GMB) ensures your business appears on Google Maps and Google Search. Businesses with a GMB profile stand out in local searches. It shows customer reviews, photos, working hours, and your contact information. As AI-Agent, we offer GMB optimization for free.
15. What is the difference between Google Ads and organic SEO? +
Google Ads are paid advertisements and give immediate results, but traffic stops when the ad budget runs out. Organic SEO is a long-term investment, giving results after 3-6 months but providing sustainable traffic. The ideal strategy is to use both: getting quick results with Google Ads in the short term, while building organic traffic with SEO in the long term.
16. What features are essential for an e-commerce site? +
Secure payment infrastructure (SSL, 3D Secure), product management system, inventory tracking, shipping integration, customer account management, cart and favorites features, mobile compatibility, and fast loading are essential. Integration with local cargo companies and customer support are also important. As AI-Agent, we offer e-commerce solutions including all these features.
17. Why is social media management important? +
Social media is a critical channel for building brand awareness, customer engagement, and increasing sales. Platforms like Instagram and Facebook are very effective. You can build brand loyalty with regular content sharing, responding to customer comments, and targeted ads. As AI-Agent, we offer social media strategy, content creation, and ad management services.
18. How does an AI chatbot benefit my business? +
AI chatbot provides 24/7 customer support, automatically answers frequently asked questions, creates appointments, and directs potential customers. It can offer features like multi-language support (Turkish, English, Russian, German) for hotels, menu information and reservation for restaurants. As AI-Agent, we develop chatbot solutions specific to your business.
19. How to create a digital menu for a restaurant? +
A digital menu is a mobile-responsive web application accessible via QR code. It can include product photos, category-based menu, price information, allergen warnings, and multi-language support. English, Russian, and German menu support is important for tourist-heavy areas. As AI-Agent, we offer QR code digital menu solutions.

As a first step to digital transformation, you should create a modern website, Google My Business profile, and social media accounts. Then you can continue with SEO work, Google Ads campaigns, and CRM systems. As AI-Agent, we offer free digital transformation consulting. Call us now, let's create a special roadmap for your business.
21. Why do local businesses need AI transformation? +
Businesses managed with traditional methods lag behind their digitalizing competitors in efficiency and speed. AI transformation provides advantages such as instant response to customer requests, automatic appointment creation, and error-free accounting processes. SMEs can compete with large corporate firms and reduce operational costs by up to 40% with AI tools.
22. How is market research done with AI? +
Traditional market research is expensive and time-consuming. Our AI agents analyze billions of data on the web to analyze consumer behaviors, competitor strategies, and industry trends. We answer questions like "Which product is searched more?" or "What do customers complain about most in competitors?" with data-driven reports, minimizing your risk in new product launches.
23. How does AI-supported inventory management work? +
Excess inventory disrupts cash flow, while lack of inventory loses customers. Our AI algorithms forecast future demand by analyzing past sales data, seasonal cycles, special days, and even weather forecasts. With systems that automatically answer "How much of which product should you order next week?", businesses reduce inventory waste rates by 25%.
24. What is dynamic pricing strategy? +
Dynamic pricing is an AI technology that automatically adjusts the price of your product or service according to demand, time, and competitor prices. It increases occupancy by optimizing the price when demand is low; and maximizes your profitability by up to 30% by increasing the price when demand is high.
25. Can I analyze my competitors with AI? +
Absolutely. Our AI tools monitor your competitors' website changes, price updates, social media strategies, and customer feedback 24/7. You get notified instantly when your competitors start a new campaign and can develop a counter-strategy. This competitive intelligence is vital for protecting and increasing your market share.
26. How is AI used in Human Resources (HR) processes? +
Scanning thousands of CVs in recruitment processes is a waste of time. AI automatically matches candidates' skills with job descriptions. Also, employee performance analysis and shift planning are optimized with AI, minimizing overtime costs and staff dissatisfaction.
27. How does AI interpret customer feedback (Sentiment Analysis)? +
It is impossible to read thousands of comments about your business one by one. Our AI sentiment analysis tools classify these comments as "Positive", "Negative", "Neutral" and report the main reasons for complaints (e.g., "Staff indifference", "High price", "Cleaning issue"). Thus, you can solve operational problems at their source.
28. What is the role of AI in financial forecasting and cash flow management? +
Our AI models forecast your future 3-6-12 month cash flow with high accuracy by analyzing your past financial data. This foresight allows you to make investment decisions (opening a new branch, hiring staff, etc.) in a data-driven and safe way.
29. What is Sales Funnel automation? +
It is the management of the process from a potential customer visiting your website to making a purchase with AI. AI sends automatic emails according to the products the customer is interested in, makes reminders via WhatsApp, and notifies your sales team about customers with the highest purchase probability (Lead Scoring). This system can triple sales conversion rates.
30. How is artificial intelligence used in supply chain management? +
AI lowers logistics costs with route optimization and predicts possible supply delays in advance, offering alternative plans. It makes logistics operations 20% more efficient for companies exporting to the world.
31. What is personalized customer experience? +
Customers prefer brands that offer personal recommendations. AI analyzes each customer's shopping history and preferences to offer personalized recommendations on your website, emails, and ads. This increases customer loyalty (LTV) and basket amount.
32. How do Fraud Detection systems work? +
Security is vital for e-commerce sites. AI algorithms detect and block suspicious transaction patterns (e.g., stolen card attempts, abnormal order quantities) in milliseconds. This prevents financial loss for your business and ensures customer security.
33. What is AI in industrial maintenance (Predictive Maintenance)? +
AI monitoring sensor data predicts that a machine will malfunction days before it breaks down (e.g., from vibration or heat change). This prevents unplanned downtimes and production losses by planning "Predictive Maintenance".
34. Can AI be integrated into my existing ERP/CRM software? +
Yes, you don't have to change your existing systems. We can integrate AI layers via API into ERP/accounting programs like Logo, Mikro, SAP, or Netsis. Thus, you can perform smart analyses on your existing data and see inventory and turnover forecasts on the screens you use.
35. How to set up AI automated reporting systems? +
Instead of wasting hours with weekly Excel reports, AI dashboards present you data live. You see the answers to questions like "What was the most profitable product last week?" on a single screen with instantly updated charts. It is the most important tool that speeds up the decision-making process for managers.

Communicating with tourists can be difficult. AI-supported instant translation systems ensure that the chatbot on your website or your WhatsApp line automatically answers the incoming message in the customer's language. You offer a local experience to a global customer base by eliminating the language barrier.

AI investment is a high-return investment. AI adaptation in businesses usually covers the investment cost (ROI) within the first 6 months. With increased operational efficiency, personnel cost savings, and sales growth, businesses using AI grow 25% faster than their competitors.
