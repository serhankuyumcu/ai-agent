Privacy Policy & GDPR

We are transparent about how we protect and process your data.

1. Overview

As AI-Agent ("Company", "we"), we attach great importance to the security and privacy of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how the information collected when you visit our website (ai-agent.com.tr) or use our services is used, stored, and protected.

By using our services, you agree to the data processing practices outlined in this policy.

2. GDPR & KVKK Compliance Statement

In accordance with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and KVKK (Turkish Data Protection Law No. 6698), as the data controller, your personal data may be processed for the purposes explained below.

Our Data Processing Purposes:

  • Providing and improving the quality of our services
  • Managing customer requests and complaints
  • Fulfilling legal obligations
  • Carrying out marketing and promotional activities (with your explicit consent)
  • Improving business processes and training AI models (with anonymized data)

3. Data Collected

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Identity Information: Name, surname.
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, device information.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with our services.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure the security of your personal data. Your data is stored on encrypted servers and protected against unauthorized access. All data transfers on our website are encrypted with an SSL certificate (Secure Socket Layer).

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze site traffic, and offer personalized content. You can reject cookies by changing your browser settings, but in this case, some features of the website may not work properly.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and KVKK, you have the following rights:

  • To learn whether your personal data is processed,
  • To request information if it has been processed,
  • To learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used appropriately,
  • To know the third parties to whom it is transferred domestically or abroad,
  • To request correction if it is incomplete or incorrectly processed.

Contact and Application

You can contact us for your questions about this policy or your requests regarding your rights under GDPR/KVKK. Our data privacy officer will respond to your application as soon as possible.
